The Traffic Light
Result Protocol

Our staged results system ensures clarity in intervention. Every assessment is categorised into one of four distinct stages, guiding educators and psychologists on the next steps.

N

Normal

Optimal emotional health. The child demonstrates robust resilience and strong supportive networks.

M

Mild

Transient stressors identified. Recommend standard pastoral care monitoring and routine check-ins.

E

Elevated

Significant emotional distress markers. Tier-2 intervention required: targeted counselling sessions.

C

Clinical

Acute psychological distress. Immediate clinical referral and comprehensive parental intervention.

Normal

Optimal emotional health. Strong resilience and supportive networks detected.
