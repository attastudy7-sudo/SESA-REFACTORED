Why It Matters

The Invisible Barriers to Learning

Traditional academic assessments often miss the emotional silent struggles that prevent students from reaching their full potential.

Silent Anxiety

Identifying students who struggle with persistent worry that impairs focus and academic performance.

Hidden Depression

Early detection of depressive symptoms that affect social integration and attendance.

Compulsive Patterns

Supporting schools in identifying OCD tendencies and intrusive thought patterns disrupting classroom participation.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Social Emotional Screening

Everything your school needs to identify, support, and track student wellbeing — in one secure platform.

Six Validated Assessments

Covering Separation Anxiety, Social Phobia, Generalised Anxiety, Panic Disorder, OCD, and Major Depressive Disorder.

Staged Results

Each assessment returns a clearly interpreted result — Normal, Mild, Elevated, or Clinical — with personalised guidance.

School Dashboard

Administrators access a dedicated dashboard to monitor student results, upload student lists, and track trends.

Private and Secure

Student data is protected. Results are confidential and only accessible to authorised school personnel.

At-Risk Early Alerts

SESA automatically flags students with Elevated or Clinical results so counsellors and administrators can intervene before issues escalate.

Easy Student Onboarding

Students join instantly using a school access code — no email required. Bulk upload via Excel is also supported for faster setup.
