6 Dimensions of Emotional Safety

Our audit structure categorises emotional well-being into distinct analytical pathways, providing a holistic view of the school's climate.

01

Cognitive Resilience

Evaluating the ability to manage stress and bounce back from academic setbacks and life challenges.

02

Relational Trust

Measuring the quality of peer-to-peer and student-teacher connections within the school ecosystem.

03

Emotional Autonomy

Assessing the student's capacity for independent emotional regulation and self-awareness.
