The Social Emotional Students' Audit (SESA) helps schools identify, understand, and support the mental and emotional health of their students - early and effectively.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Social Emotional Screening

Six Validated Assessments

Covering Separation Anxiety, Social Phobia, Generalised Anxiety, Panic Disorder, OCD, and Major Depressive Disorder.

Staged Results

Each assessment returns a clearly interpreted result - Normal, Mild, Elevated, or Clinical - with personalised guidance.

School Dashboard

Administrators access a dedicated dashboard to monitor student results, upload student lists, and track trends.

Private and Secure

Student data is protected. Results are confidential and only accessible to authorised school personnel.

GES Aligned

Aligned with Ghana Education Service Policy

SESA supports the GES School Health Policy by providing schools with structured, evidence-informed tools to identify and respond to students' social-emotional needs — enabling schools to meet their wellbeing reporting obligations.
