School Registration

How to register your school and activate your SESA account.

1

Register your school

Go to the SESA homepage and click Register My School. Fill in your school name, administrator name, email, and a secure password. Click Register School.

2

Await activation

After registration, your account will be reviewed by FOPA DILLIGENT CONSULT. Student upload access is enabled only after payment confirmation. You will be notified when your account is activated.

3

Log in to your School Portal

Once activated, go to School Administrator Login on the login page. Enter your administrator name and password to access your dashboard.

4

Share your access code with students

Your dashboard shows a unique School Access Code and QR Code. Students can use either to join your school without needing an Excel upload — useful for individual sign-ups.

{# How usernames work #}
Usernames are auto-generated — no column needed.

Each student gets a username in the format firstname.lastname.schoolcode (e.g. ama.mensah.accrah). If that username is already taken, a number is added automatically.

{# Step by step #}

Step-by-Step Upload

1

Open the template

Download the template above and open it in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets. Student data starts at row 5. Delete the 5 sample rows before uploading.

2

Fill in student data

Enter one student per row. Only first_name and last_name are required. Fill optional columns where available — the more data, the richer the dashboard reports.

3

Save as .xlsx

Save the file in Excel format (.xlsx or .xls). CSV files are not accepted. In Google Sheets: File → Download → Microsoft Excel.

4

Upload on your dashboard

In your school dashboard, find the Upload Students section. Click Choose File, select your .xlsx file, then click Upload.

5

Confirm the result

A success message will show how many accounts were created and how many rows were skipped. Students can now log in with their username and temporary password.

Upload is only available once your subscription is activated.

If you see a warning about upload being disabled, contact FOPA DILLIGENT CONSULT to confirm your payment status.

Join by Code (Alternative Method)

Students can join your school individually using your access code or QR code — no Excel needed.

Access Code

Your school has a unique Access Code visible on your dashboard. Share it with students. They go to the SESA homepage, click Join with Code, enter the code, and create their account — automatically linked to your school.

QR Code

Your dashboard also shows a QR Code that students can scan with their phone. This takes them directly to the sign-up page pre-linked to your school — no code typing required. Print it and display it in your school.

When to use the code method vs. Excel upload

Use Excel upload for bulk onboarding at the start of term. Use the access code / QR code for new students joining mid-term, or for schools that prefer students to create their own accounts.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and how to fix them.

"Upload is not enabled" warning
Your school subscription has not been activated yet. Contact FOPA DILLIGENT CONSULT to confirm your payment and request activation.
"Missing required columns: first_name, last_name"
Your column headers don't match exactly. Make sure the headers in row 4 of your file are spelled first_name and last_name — all lowercase, with underscores, no spaces.
"Invalid file type" error
Only .xlsx and .xls files are accepted. If you are using Google Sheets, go to File → Download → Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) before uploading.
Some rows were skipped
Rows are skipped when first_name or last_name is empty. Check your file for blank rows or rows with missing names and re-upload just those students.
Level or gender not showing correctly
These fields only accept exact values. For level: primaryschool, middleschool, highschool, or university. For gender: male, female, or other. Any other value will be ignored.
A student forgot their password
Their initial password is their Student ID from the upload file. If no Student ID was provided, their password is their username. You can view student usernames from your dashboard. Students can reset their password from the login page.
Students can't find the login page
Direct students to the SESA homepage and tell them to click Sign In. They log in with their username (not email) and their temporary password.
Still need help?

Contact FOPA DILLIGENT CONSULT for support with your school account, subscription, or any technical issues not covered here.
